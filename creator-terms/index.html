Who Is a Creator

A "Creator" is any user with access to Studio — the set of tools for designing, configuring, and publishing AI avatars on Avatar Arcade. Creator access is granted at our discretion and may be limited during early access periods.

What You Build

Using Studio, you can create avatar configurations that include:

  • Character name, tagline, and personality prompt
  • Voice selection and configuration
  • Visual appearance (portraits, avatar rendering style)
  • Background audio and ambient settings
  • Trailer recordings that showcase the avatar

Together, these elements form your "Creator Content."

Publishing and Visibility

Draft vs. Published

Avatars start as private drafts visible only to you. Publishing makes them discoverable by all users. You can unpublish at any time, which returns the avatar to a private state.

Content Screening

When you activate an avatar for the first time, it goes through an automated content screen. If something is flagged, you'll see a clear explanation and can edit before retrying. See our Community Guidelines for what's checked.

Suspension

If a published avatar is reported and found to violate the Community Guidelines or Terms of Service, it may be suspended from public access. During suspension:

  • The avatar is hidden from browse, search, and collections
  • New conversations cannot start
  • Active conversations continue until they end naturally
  • You'll see the reason in Studio (but not the reporter's identity)
  • Only an admin resolution can restore or remove the avatar

Removing Your Content

  • Unpublish — hides the avatar from public discovery. You can do this anytime from Studio.
  • Archive — removes the avatar from your Studio workspace. Underlying data is retained for billing, analytics, and compliance purposes.
  • Full deletion — email support@avatararcade.ai. We process deletion requests within 30 days, except where retention is required for billing records, legal compliance, or active disputes.

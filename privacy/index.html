Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 23, 2026

Overview

Avatar Arcade ("the App") is operated by Ink Sky Media LLC ("we", "us", "our"). This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

Conversations with AI avatars are not private. When you talk to an avatar, your voice is transcribed, your conversation is logged, and sessions may be recorded. We use this data to operate, improve, and develop the App. Our team may review conversations for quality and safety. If you wouldn't say it in a room full of friendly robots taking notes — maybe don't say it here either.

Information We Collect

Account Information

When you sign in using Google or Apple, we receive your name and email address from the authentication provider. We do not receive or store your password.

Conversation Data

When you have a conversation with an AI avatar, we collect and store:

  • Transcripts — Your spoken words are transcribed to text and stored along with the avatar's responses. Transcripts are linked to your account.
  • Session recordings — Audio and video from avatar sessions may be recorded and stored for clip creation, content features, and quality improvement.
  • Session metadata — Duration, avatar configuration, AI model usage, performance metrics, and error data.

Voice and Audio

During conversations, your voice is streamed in real-time to speech-to-text services for processing. Voice data is sent to third-party AI providers to generate avatar responses. Audio streams may be recorded as described above.

Usage Data

We collect information about how you use the App, including session frequency, feature interactions, navigation patterns, and engagement metrics.

Creator Data

If you use Studio tools to create avatars, we collect the inputs you provide — including personality prompts, voice configurations, image prompts, and creative direction. Creator sessions with the Avatar Assistant are logged like any other conversation.

Purchase Information

In-app purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. We receive confirmation of purchases and manage your credit balance, but do not have access to your payment card details.

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and operate the App, including processing conversations with AI avatars
  • Generate clips, trailers, and other content features from session data
  • Monitor conversation quality and safety
  • Analyze usage patterns and AI model performance to improve the service
  • Debug issues and maintain platform reliability
  • Manage your account and credit balance
  • Communicate with you about your account or the App
  • Create anonymized or aggregated datasets for research and product development

Human Review

Our team may review conversation transcripts, session recordings, and AI model outputs for the following purposes:

  • Quality assurance — ensuring avatar responses meet our standards
  • Safety monitoring — identifying harmful, abusive, or policy-violating content
  • AI improvement — understanding model behavior and improving response quality
  • Debugging — diagnosing technical issues with avatar sessions

Team members with access to conversation data are subject to confidentiality obligations.

Third-Party Services

We use the following categories of third-party services to operate the App. Each service receives only the data necessary for its function:

Infrastructure

  • Clerk — Authentication (sign-in with Google/Apple)
  • Convex — Backend database and server functions
  • LiveKit — Real-time audio and video streaming
  • Cloudflare R2 — Media storage (avatars, recordings, generated assets)

AI Providers

  • Speech-to-text providers (e.g., Deepgram) — Process your voice into text
  • Language model providers (e.g., OpenAI, Google) — Generate avatar responses
  • Text-to-speech providers (e.g., Cartesia, ElevenLabs) — Generate avatar voice audio
  • Avatar rendering providers — Generate visual avatar video

Your voice and conversation content is sent to these providers in real-time to power avatar conversations. Each provider has its own data handling practices.

Analytics and Monitoring

  • PostHog — Product analytics (usage events, feature engagement, linked to your account identifier)
  • Langfuse — AI observability (model performance, latency, token usage, session identifiers)

Payments

  • RevenueCat — In-app purchase management and subscription analytics

Data Retention

  • Account information — Retained while your account is active, deleted within 30 days of a deletion request.
  • Conversation transcripts — Retained for the life of your account. Used for content features (clips) and service improvement.
  • Session recordings — Retained for the life of your account. May be used for clips, trailers, and quality review.
  • Usage analytics — Retained according to each analytics provider's standard retention period.
  • Billing records — Retained as required by law, even after account deletion.

When you delete your account, we remove your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required for billing, legal compliance, or ongoing dispute resolution. Anonymized or aggregated data derived from your usage may be retained indefinitely.

Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us immediately.

Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including encryption in transit (TLS/SSL), secure authentication, and access controls on stored data. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing
  • Request a copy of your data in a portable format
  • Opt out of certain data uses (contact us for details)

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@avatararcade.ai. We will respond within 30 days.

California Residents

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), California residents have additional rights including the right to know what personal information is collected, the right to request deletion, and the right to opt out of the "sale" of personal information. We do not sell your personal information in the traditional sense, but sharing data with third-party analytics services may constitute a "sale" under CCPA's broad definition. To opt out, contact us at privacy@avatararcade.ai.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the App or by email. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

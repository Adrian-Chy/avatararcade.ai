Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 19, 2026

Overview

Avatar Arcade ("the App") is operated by Ink Sky Media LLC ("we", "us", "our"). This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Account Information: When you sign in using Google or Apple, we receive your name and email address from the authentication provider. We do not receive or store your password.

Usage Data: We collect information about how you use the App, including session duration and feature usage, to improve our service.

Voice and Audio: During avatar conversations, your voice is processed in real-time by our speech-to-text service to generate responses. Audio is streamed and processed transiently — we do not store recordings of your conversations.

Purchase Information: In-app purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. We receive confirmation of purchases but do not have access to your payment details.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the App
  • To process your conversations with AI avatars
  • To manage your account and credit balance
  • To improve the App based on usage patterns
  • To communicate with you about your account or the App

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate the App:

  • Clerk — Authentication (sign-in with Google/Apple)
  • LiveKit — Real-time video and audio streaming
  • RevenueCat — In-app purchase management
  • Convex — Backend data storage
  • AI model providers — Speech recognition, language processing, text-to-speech, and avatar rendering

Each service has its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review them.

Data Retention

We retain your account information for as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.

Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us.

Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including encryption in transit (TLS/SSL) and secure authentication. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Your Rights

You may:

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing
  • Request a copy of your data in a portable format

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the App or by email.
